Why Verum

Everything an election needs, nothing it doesn't.

Three things matter most when people vote online: their ballot stays private, the site doesn't slow them down, and the result can be checked.

Secure

Ballots are encrypted the moment they're submitted and stored separately from voter identity.

Fast

Registering, verifying, and voting takes under two minutes, on any device, no app required.

Transparent

A public audit log lets anyone confirm the count is correct, without exposing a single vote.

The process

Three steps from sign-up to result.

1

Register and verify

Sign up with your name and voter or student ID. We check your eligibility right away.

2

Review and vote

3

See the result

Watch turnout and results update once polls close, backed by a public audit trail.
